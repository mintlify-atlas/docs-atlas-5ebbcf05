Regions Unexplored adds **5 specialized cave biomes** that transform underground exploration with glowing plants, crystal formations, massive mushrooms, and volcanic activity.

## Cave biome features

Underground biomes appear naturally during world generation beneath the surface, each with distinctive visual effects and resources.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Unique vegetation" icon="seedling">
    Each cave biome features exclusive plants that only grow underground, from glowing mushrooms to aquatic flora.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ambient particles" icon="sparkles">
    Atmospheric particle effects create immersive environments, from prismatic sparkles to volcanic ash.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom lighting" icon="lightbulb">
    Bioluminescent plants and crystal formations provide natural light sources in the darkness.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Specialized resources" icon="gem">
    Gather unique materials found nowhere else, including prismarite clusters and mineral formations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Lush cave variants

These underground biomes feature water, vegetation, and vibrant ecosystems.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ancient Delta" icon="droplet">
    A lush underground delta featuring ancient aquatic vegetation and mineral-rich pools. The water glows with an otherworldly cyan color, and the foliage displays deep green tones with purple grass accents.
    
    **Temperature:** 1.125 (warm) | **Precipitation:** Yes
    
    ### Features
    - Mineral pools with unique geological formations
    - Bladed grass providing ground coverage
    - Corpse flowers - rare and unusual plants
    - Duskmelons - edible underground crops
    - Dusktrap plants - carnivorous vegetation
    - Dropleaf plants near water
    - Clay deposits for building materials
    
    ### Atmosphere
    - **Water color:** Cyan (-13369345)
    - **Foliage:** Deep green (-10118056)
    - **Grass:** Purple tinted (16318340)
    - **Music:** Lush caves ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    Spawns axolotls, tropical fish, and standard peaceful mobs. Safe for exploration with aquatic wildlife.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bioshroom Caves" icon="mushroom">
    Massive underground mushroom forests featuring giant bioshroms in vibrant blue, green, and pink colors. Creates an alien landscape with bioluminescent fungal growth.
    
    **Temperature:** 1.25 (very warm) | **Precipitation:** Yes
    
    ### Features
    - **Giant green bioshroms** - massive mushroom trees
    - **Giant blue bioshroms** - towering fungal structures
    - Cave grass and tall cave grass coverage
    - Blue, green, and pink bioshroom varieties
    - Unique fungal ecosystem
    
    ### Atmosphere
    - **Water color:** Standard blue
    - **Foliage:** Deep green (-11093361)
    - **Grass:** Dark green (-11093410)
    - **Music:** Lush caves ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    Spawns axolotls, tropical fish, and peaceful creatures. The fungal environment supports unique wildlife.
    
    <Note>
      Bioshroms are exclusive to this biome and provide unique decorative blocks when harvested.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Dry cave variants

Dangerous underground biomes with unique geological features.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Prismachasm" icon="gem">
    A crystalline underground chasm filled with prismarite formations that sparkle with magical particles. Features purple-tinted vegetation and glowing crystal clusters.
    
    **Temperature:** 0.925 (cool) | **Precipitation:** Yes
    
    ### Features
    - **Prismarite clusters** - valuable crystal formations
    - **Hanging prismarite** - ceiling-mounted crystals
    - Prismoss sprouts with purple coloration
    - Cave hyssop flowering plants
    - Ambient prismarite sparkle particles
    
    ### Atmosphere
    - **Water color:** Standard blue
    - **Foliage:** Deep purple (-16737793)
    - **Grass:** Purple tinted (-6625354)
    - **Particles:** Prismarite sparkles (0.001 density)
    - **Music:** Dripstone caves ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    Standard hostile cave mobs including zombies, skeletons, and creepers. Exercise caution while mining crystals.
    
    <Warning>
      Prismachasm is one of the rarest cave biomes. Mark coordinates when you find one to return for prismarite farming.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Redstone Caves" icon="bolt">
    Underground caverns naturally rich in redstone with pointed redstone formations growing from floors and ceilings. The entire biome has a reddish tint.
    
    **Temperature:** 0.8 (cool) | **Precipitation:** Yes
    
    ### Features
    - **Pointed redstone** - natural redstone stalactites and stalagmites
    - Enhanced redstone ore generation
    - Reddish foliage and grass
    - Standard cave features with redstone theme
    
    ### Atmosphere
    - **Water color:** Standard blue
    - **Foliage:** Dark red (-6044317)
    - **Grass:** Dark red (-6044317)
    - **Music:** Dripstone caves ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    Standard hostile cave spawns. The abundant redstone makes this biome valuable for technical builds.
    
    <Tip>
      Redstone caves provide excellent redstone farming opportunities. Set up mining outposts to harvest the abundant ore.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Scorching Caves" icon="fire">
    Volcanic underground caverns with active lava flows, basalt formations, and ash particles. The hottest and most dangerous cave biome.
    
    **Temperature:** 2.0 (scorching) | **Precipitation:** No
    
    ### Features
    - **Overworld lava deltas** - extensive lava pools
    - **Basalt blobs** - natural basalt formations
    - **Ash vents** - volcanic activity
    - **Lava falls** - dramatic flowing lava
    - White ash particle effects
    
    ### Atmosphere
    - **Water color:** Standard blue (rare)
    - **Foliage:** Dark ash (-8949914)
    - **Grass:** Burnt color (-8621472)
    - **Particles:** White ash (0.1 density)
    - **Music:** Deep dark ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    Standard hostile mobs spawn despite the heat. Fire resistance recommended for exploration.
    
    <Warning>
      Bring fire resistance potions when exploring scorching caves. Lava is abundant and dangerous.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Locating cave biomes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use locate command">
    Use `/locatebiome regions_unexplored:biome_name` to find specific cave biomes. Note that cave biomes may be far from spawn.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore below Y=0">
    Cave biomes generate in deepslate layers. Explore underground cave systems below Y-level 0 for the best chance of discovery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Look for visual cues">
    Unique particle effects, lighting, and vegetation colors indicate you've entered a special cave biome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark coordinates">
    Cave biomes can be rare. Always mark coordinates when you find one for future resource gathering.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Mining strategies

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Ancient Delta">
    Harvest unique aquatic plants and mineral pools. Bring water breathing potions for extended exploration. Axolotls can be captured for breeding.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bioshroom Caves">
    Collect all three bioshroom colors for decorative blocks. Use silk touch to gather bioshroom blocks intact. Plant small bioshroms for renewable resources.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Prismachasm">
    Mine prismarite clusters with any pickaxe. Hanging clusters require careful mining to avoid falls. Sparkle particles help locate clusters in darkness.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Redstone Caves">
    Optimize redstone farming by mining pointed redstone formations. Bring fortune pickaxes for maximum yield. Ideal for stocking up on redstone for builds.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Scorching Caves">
    Prioritize fire resistance and safe pathing. Collect basalt using any pickaxe. Mine magma blocks for bubble columns. Avoid breaking lava source blocks.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Cave biomes can overlap with vanilla cave types like dripstone and lush caves, creating hybrid areas with features from both.
</Note>
