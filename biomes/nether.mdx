Regions Unexplored expands the Nether with **5 new biomes**, each featuring distinct vegetation, particle effects, and hostile atmospheres that diversify the hellish dimension.

## Nether biome features

All Nether biomes share extreme heat and hostile conditions but offer unique resources and aesthetics.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="No rain" icon="sun">
    All Nether biomes have no precipitation and maintain temperature of 2.0 (extreme heat).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unique flora" icon="seedling">
    Each biome features exclusive Nether plants, from glowing earlight to toxic fungal growth.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Particle effects" icon="sparkles">
    Atmospheric particles create immersive environments, from warped spores to crimson spores.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hostile mobs" icon="skull">
    Dangerous mobs spawn including piglins, hoglins, wither skeletons, and zombified piglins.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Dark Nether biomes

Biomes with darker color palettes and ominous atmospheres.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Blackstone Basin" icon="mountain">
    A dark valley filled with blackstone formations and cobalt vegetation glowing with ethereal blue light. Features obsidian spires rising from the basin floor.
    
    **Temperature:** 2.0 (extreme heat) | **Precipitation:** No
    
    ### Features
    - **Obsidian spires** - tall dark formations
    - **Cobalt trees** - unique Nether wood type
    - **Cobalt roots** - underground root systems
    - **Cobalt earlight** - glowing blue plants
    - **Tall cobalt earlight** - larger light sources
    - **Hanging earlight** - ceiling vegetation
    - **Blackstone clusters** - natural blackstone formations
    - Enhanced blackstone blob generation
    
    ### Atmosphere
    - **Sky/fog color:** Dark blue (395547)
    - **Water color:** Blue-green (5463027)
    - **Foliage:** Blue-green (5463027)
    - **Grass:** Blue-green (5463027)
    - **Particles:** Warped spores (0.02 density)
    - **Sounds:** Crimson forest loop with warped mood
    - **Music:** Crimson forest ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    - **Zombified piglins** - Common (25 weight)
    - **Wither skeletons** - Common (20 weight)
    - **Enderman** - Rare (1 weight)
    - **Striders** - Very common (60 weight)
    
    <Tip>
      Blackstone basin provides excellent blackstone and cobalt wood farming. The cobalt earlight serves as a unique light source for Nether builds.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Infernal Holt" icon="fire">
    A hellish woodland featuring brim willow trees and volcanic plant life. White ash falls constantly from the burning atmosphere.
    
    **Temperature:** 2.0 (extreme heat) | **Precipitation:** No
    
    ### Features
    - **Brim willow trees** - standard and tall variants
    - **Brimsprout** - small Nether plants
    - **Dorcel** - unique vegetation
    - **Brim flames** - natural fire sources
    - **Brimwood shrubs** - ground coverage
    - Standard Nether features (glowstone, magma, mushrooms)
    
    ### Atmosphere
    - **Sky/fog color:** Dark orange (3479565)
    - **Water color:** Orange-brown (3479565)
    - **Foliage:** Burnt orange (7295817)
    - **Grass:** Burnt orange (7295817)
    - **Particles:** White ash (0.03 density)
    - **Sounds:** Basalt deltas loop with warped mood
    - **Music:** Basalt deltas ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    - **Hoglins** - Very common (9 weight)
    - **Piglins** - Common (5 weight)
    - **Zombified piglins** - Rare (1 weight)
    - **Striders** - Very common (60 weight)
    
    <Warning>
      Hoglins are aggressive and spawn frequently. Bring warped fungus or wear gold armor to avoid piglin attacks.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Redstone Abyss" icon="bolt">
    A vast Nether wasteland rich in redstone with pointed redstone formations. Features reddish atmosphere and crimson particle effects.
    
    **Temperature:** 2.0 (extreme heat) | **Precipitation:** No
    
    ### Features
    - **Nether pointed redstone** - unique redstone formations
    - Enhanced redstone-themed terrain
    - Standard Nether features (glowstone, fire, magma)
    - Default Nether ores
    
    ### Atmosphere
    - **Sky/fog color:** Dark red (5439488)
    - **Water color:** Red (10623252)
    - **Foliage:** Red (10623252)
    - **Grass:** Red (10623252)
    - **Particles:** Crimson spores (0.025 density)
    - **Sounds:** Nether wastes ambient
    - **Music:** Nether wastes ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    - **Zombified piglins** - Very common (100 weight)
    - **Striders** - Very common (60 weight)
    - Minimal other mob spawns
    
    <Note>
      Redstone abyss provides redstone resources in the Nether, useful for Nether-based technical builds.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Bright Nether biomes

Biomes with lighter colors and glowing vegetation.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Glistering Meadow" icon="sparkles">

    **Temperature:** 2.0 (extreme heat) | **Precipitation:** No
    
    ### Features
    - **Glistering meadow rock** - unique stone formations
    - **Glister bulb** - large glowing plants
    - **Glistering sprout** - small light sources
    - **Glistering fern** - medium vegetation
    - **Glistering bloom** - flowering plants
    - **Glister spire** - tall formations
    - **Glistering ivy** - hanging vines
    
    ### Atmosphere
    - **Sky/fog color:** Cyan-blue (4328552)
    - **Water color:** Bright cyan (12058781)
    - **Foliage:** Bright cyan (12058781)
    - **Grass:** Bright cyan (12058781)
    - **Particles:** Warped spores (0.02 density)
    - **Sounds:** Crimson forest loop with warped mood
    - **Music:** Warped forest ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    - **Ghasts** - Common (5 weight)
    - **Hoglins** - Common (5 weight)
    - **Striders** - Common (40 weight)
    - **Enderman** - Rare (1 weight)
    
    <Tip>
      The abundant glowing plants make glistering meadow ideal for Nether bases. Harvest glister vegetation for unique decorative blocks.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mycotoxic Undergrowth" icon="biohazard">

    - **Particles:** Mycotoxic spores (0.01 density)
    - **Sounds:** Warped forest ambient
    - **Music:** Warped forest ambient
    
    ### Mobs
    - **Zombified piglins** - Very common (100 weight)
    - **Piglins** - Common (5 weight)
    - **Striders** - Very common (60 weight)
    
    <Warning>

    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Locating Nether biomes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build a Nether highway">
    Create safe tunnels at Y=120 in the Nether ceiling or pathways across the lava ocean to explore efficiently.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use locate command">
    Use `/locatebiome regions_unexplored:biome_name` to find specific Nether biomes and their coordinates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Look for color changes">
    Each Nether biome has distinct fog and foliage colors. Watch for color shifts as you travel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Listen for audio cues">
    Different ambient sounds and music indicate when you've crossed into a new biome.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Resource gathering

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Blackstone Basin">
    Harvest cobalt wood for unique building blocks. Mine blackstone clusters efficiently. Collect cobalt earlight for lighting. Bring a warped fungus on a stick for strider transportation.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Infernal Holt">
    Collect brimwood from brim willow trees. Harvest brimsprout and dorcel for decoration. Wear gold armor to pacify piglins. Be cautious of frequent hoglin spawns.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Glistering Meadow">
    Gather all seven types of glister vegetation for comprehensive decoration options. Mine glister spires and rocks. Watch for ghasts in the open terrain.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mycotoxic Undergrowth">
    Harvest giant yellow bioshroms for unique fungal blocks. Collect mycotoxic plants carefully. Extra glowstone makes this biome valuable for lighting materials.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Redstone Abyss">
    Mine pointed redstone formations for Nether-accessible redstone. Relatively safe with fewer dangerous mobs. Good for establishing Nether outposts.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Survival tips

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Fire resistance" icon="fire-extinguisher">
    Always carry fire resistance potions when exploring Nether biomes. Lava is abundant in all biomes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gold armor" icon="shield">
    Wear at least one piece of gold armor to prevent piglin aggression in biomes where they spawn.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build safe platforms" icon="cube">
    Create cobblestone platforms for safe navigation across lava oceans between biomes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mark coordinates" icon="location-dot">
    Save coordinates of interesting biomes. The Nether is vast and biomes can be hard to relocate.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  All Nether biomes maintain the standard 8:1 travel ratio with the Overworld, making them useful for long-distance transportation systems.
</Note>
