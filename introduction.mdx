## Transform your Minecraft world

Regions Unexplored is a comprehensive biome mod that adds 71 unique biomes to both the Overworld and Nether dimensions. From lush magnolia forests to fiery volcanic wastes, each biome features custom blocks, plants, trees, and world generation that seamlessly integrates with vanilla Minecraft.

## Key features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="71 unique biomes" icon="mountain">
    Explore diverse environments spanning the Overworld and Nether, including:
    - **Forests**: Autumnal Maple Forest, Eucalyptus Forest, Redwoods, Blackwood Taiga
    - **Wetlands**: Bayou, Fen, Fungal Fen, Willow Forest
    - **Plains & Savannas**: Clover Plains, Prairie, Baobab Savanna, Steppe
    - **Mountains**: Arid Mountains, Chalk Cliffs, Towering Cliffs
    - **Caves**: Bioshroom Caves, Prismachasm, Redstone Caves
    - **Nether**: Ancient Delta, Ashen Woodland, Blackstone Basin, Glistering Meadow
    
    Each biome features unique terrain generation, vegetation, and atmospheric effects.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Custom wood types" icon="tree">
    Craft with 15+ new wood types, each with full block sets:
    - Complete furniture sets (stairs, slabs, fences, doors, trapdoors)
    - Unique textures and colors for each wood type
    - Custom tree growers supporting small, large, and mega trees
    - Includes: Baobab, Blackwood, Brimwood, Cypress, Eucalyptus, Joshua, Kapok, Larch, Magnolia, Maple, Palm, Pine, Redwood, Socotra, and Willow
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Diverse flora" icon="seedling">
    Discover hundreds of new plants and flowers:
    - Unique flowers: Waratah, Tsubaki, Hyacinth, Coneflowers, Trillium
    - Functional plants: Salmonberry bushes, Tassel plants
    - Fantasy vegetation: Bioshrooms (Pink, Blue, Green, Yellow), Mycotoxic flora
    - Aquatic plants: Magnolia flowers, Water edge vegetation
    - Ground cover: Moss patches, Prismoss, Stone buds
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Natural blocks & materials" icon="cube">
    Build with new natural blocks:
    - Prismarite crystals and clusters for decorative builds
    - Pointed redstone formations in caves and Nether
    - Volcanic stone types including smoldering variants
    - Custom rock formations and boulders
    - Unique ground blocks for each biome
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Advanced world generation" icon="globe">
    Powered by Biolith and Lithostitched for seamless integration:
    - Biomes blend naturally with vanilla Minecraft terrain
    - Custom noise-based feature placement for realistic distributions
    - Lava deltas, mineral pools, and geothermal features
    - Shipwreck variants with biome-specific wood palettes
    - Configurable biome placement and rarity
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
