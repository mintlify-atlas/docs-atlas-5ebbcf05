## Food mechanics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Salmonberry bushes" icon="seedling">
    **Growth:** Similar to sweet berry bushes
    **Stages:** Multiple growth stages
    **Harvest:** Right-click mature bush to collect berries
    **Properties:**
    - Flammable
    - Random tick growth
    - No collision when growing
    - Sweet berry bush sounds
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Duskmelon" icon="melon">
    **Growth:** Vine-based like regular melons
    **Special notes:**
    - Causes blindness effect when eaten
    - Higher saturation than regular melon
    - Found in specific biomes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Hanging earlight" icon="vine">
    **Location:** Nether (Cobalt biome)
    **Growth:** Hangs downward like weeping vines
    **Properties:**
    - Emits light (level 14)
    - Edible fruit
    - Low chance of glowing effect
    - Random tick growth
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Meadow sage" icon="flower">
    **Type:** Tall plant (2 blocks)
    **Special notes:**
    - Only tall plant that's edible
    - 50% chance of healing
    - Low hunger restoration
    - Found in meadow biomes
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Obtaining food

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the plant source">
    Locate the plant in its natural biome:
    - Salmonberries: Various biomes
    - Duskmelon: Specific overworld biomes
    - Hanging earlight: Cobalt nether biome
    - Meadow sage: Meadow biomes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for maturity">
    Most food plants need time to grow:
    - Berry bushes have visible growth stages
    - Melons need to generate fruit blocks
    - Vines grow downward over time
  </Step>

  <Step title="Harvest carefully">
    Collection methods:
    - **Berries**: Right-click mature bushes
    - **Melons**: Break the fruit block
    - **Earlight fruit**: Break the hanging fruit
    - **Sage**: Break the plant
  </Step>

  <Step title="Consider replanting">
    Ensure sustainable food sources:
    - Collect extra berries to plant new bushes
    - Leave some plants to spread naturally
    - Use bonemeal to speed up growth
  </Step>
</Steps>
