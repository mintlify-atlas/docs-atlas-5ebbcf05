## Wood sets

Complete wood sets with full building blocks, functional blocks, and decorative elements.

<Info>
Each wood set typically includes: log, stripped log, wood, stripped wood, planks, stairs, slab, fence, fence gate, door, trapdoor, button, pressure plate, sign, and hanging sign. Some sets also include boats.
</Info>

## Natural sets

Trees with leaves, saplings, and natural variants (branches, shrubs, beards).

<Accordion title="What are natural sets?" icon="info">
  Natural sets include the organic blocks for each tree type:
  - **Leaves** - Foliage blocks with unique colors and particles
  - **Saplings** - Young trees that grow into full trees
  - **Branches** - Decorative branch blocks
  - **Shrubs** - Small bush variants
  - **Beards** - Hanging moss-like growths
</Accordion>

## Branches and decorative

<Info>
Branches are decorative blocks that can be placed like bamboo. Shrubs are small bush variants of their parent trees.
</Info>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Which trees have branches?">
    Trees with branch blocks:
    - Acacia, Birch, Cherry, Dark Oak, Jungle, Mangrove, Oak, Spruce
    - Baobab, Blackwood, Cypress, Dead, Eucalyptus, Kapok, Larch, Magnolia, Maple, Mauve, Pine, Redwood, Silver Birch, Socotra, Willow
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Which trees have shrubs?">
    Trees with shrub variants:
    - All vanilla trees
    - All modded trees except Small Oak
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Which trees have beards?">
    Trees with hanging beards:
    - Joshua
    - Palm
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
